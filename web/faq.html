Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking slot?

Log in to your ParkEase account, navigate to the dashboard, and select "Book a Slot." Choose your location, date, and time, then confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation once the slot is reserved.


Can I borrow a parking slot from a friend?

Yes! Use the "Borrow Slot" feature in the app. Enter your friend's unique slot-sharing code, and if the slot is available, you can claim it for your use.


How can I view my booking history?

Go to "Your Activity" from the menu. This section displays all your past and upcoming bookings, including details like location, date, and duration.


What happens if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel a booking from the "Your Activity" page. Select the booking and click "Cancel." Refunds, if applicable, depend on the parking provider's policy.


How do I contact support for issues?

Use the "Contact Us" page or submit a question in the help box below. Our team will respond within 24-48 hours.
